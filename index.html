About

Our crop disease detection web application offers a straightforward solution for farmers, enabling them to quickly identify and address plant diseases. Specifically designed to detect diseases in potatoes, grapes, apples, and strawberries, our user-friendly platform empowers farmers of all sizes to safeguard their crops effectively. By utilizing this tool, farmers can enhance their yield and contribute to sustainable agriculture practices.
